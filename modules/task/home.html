Welcome to Integrated Sleep Health

Providing expert care in sleep health.

Integrated Sleep Health provides expert help for patients with all different types of sleep disorders. Our philosophy is patient-centred, grounded in our academic practice and clinical research experience. We are affiliated with Australia's leading sleep research centre and are part of a network of sleep experts in different clinical sub-specialties, including clinical psychology, nutrition and exercise.


We talk to patients, we don't sell machines.
